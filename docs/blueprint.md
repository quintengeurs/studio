# **App Name**: GreenTrack Operative

## Core Features:

- User and Role Management: Create and manage user accounts with distinct roles (e.g., operative, supervisor, master) and associated permissions.
- Asset Register: Maintain a comprehensive inventory of parks and green space assets, including details, location, and condition.
- Issue Reporting and Resolution: Operatives can raise issues, assign them to specific users or teams, track their status, and mark them as resolved.
- AI-Enhanced Issue Details: Utilize an AI tool to automatically clarify or enrich reported issue descriptions, suggesting relevant asset categories or potential solutions to assist in allocation.
- Task Card Management: Create 'task card' style work assignments, detailing objectives, due dates, and assigning them to individual operatives, with status tracking.
- Performance Dashboards: Provide a master account and supervisors with dashboards visualizing asset status, open issues, task completion rates, and operative workload.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme, reflecting the natural and fresh environment of green spaces, ensuring clarity for operatives.
- Primary color: A balanced and fresh medium-dark green (#3D9947), representing nature and growth, providing good contrast on light backgrounds.
- Background color: An extremely light, almost off-white green (#F2F7F3), derived from the primary hue, to offer a clean and unobtrusive canvas.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet earthy yellow-green (#BBE36F), analogous to the primary, used to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and important statuses.
- Headline and short text font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for its modern, friendly, and legible appearance, suitable for headings and interface labels.
- Body and data font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its objective, highly readable quality, perfect for task descriptions, asset details, and dashboard data tables.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons that represent parks infrastructure, issue types, and task actions for quick comprehension.
- Clean, grid-based layouts for dashboards and task cards to ensure efficient information consumption and navigation for field operatives.
